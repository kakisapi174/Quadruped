Download the file "Remot_Quadruped.apk" on your mobile device (it has to be an android)

Connect the power supply to the robot so that the HC-05 module is blinking, once it's blinking pair the HC-05 module to your device.

*NOTE:
- Make sure that your device gives access to nearby devices for the Remot_Quadruped app

Open the Remot_Quadruped app, then click the "quadbot" button and select the HC-05 module that you paired.

once connected than the remote is now of use.

*DISCLAIMER:
- clicking the button is enough, there is no need to hold it down since there is still a considerable amount of delay

Enjoy
